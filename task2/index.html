About Environment

Introduction

The environment refers to the natural world that sorroundings.including air, water, land, plants, animals,

It provides everything necessary for life, such as food, air, and shelter.

Protecting the environment is crucial for the survival of all living beings.

Components of the environments.

  • Lithosphere
  • Hydrosphere
  • Atmosphere
  • Biosphere

It includes things created by humans such as buildings, roads, vehicles, industries, and cities.

Importance of the environment

Supports life:Provides air, water, food, and resources.

Maintain ecological balance:Ensures the survival of species and natural cycles.

Promotes health and well being:A clean environment contributes to physical and mental health.

Economic value:Natural resources are essential for agriculture, industry, and tourism.
