green technology and sustainable innovation

green technology focuses on creating products and processes that reduce environmental impact and promote sustainability.

It relies on renewable sources like solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power instead of fossil fuels to reduce carbon emissions.

The use of biodegradable, recyclable, or renewable materials reduces pollution and conserves natural resources.

Green tech promotes recycling, composting, and waste-to-energy systems to minimize landfill use and pollution

echnologies like water recycling systems and smart irrigation help preserve clean water resources.

Green buildings use sustainable materials, energy-efficient designs, and renewable energy systems to lower environmental impact.

About social media

Social media refers to online platforms and apps that allow people to create, share, and exchange information, ideas, and content (like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, X, and YouTube).

It connects people across the world instantly, breaking geographical barriers and making communication faster and easier

Social media is a major source of news, education, and awareness on global issues, trends, and events.

Users can express opinions, creativity, and individuality through posts, videos, and stories.

Social media helps spread awareness and organize campaigns for social, political, and environmental causes.
