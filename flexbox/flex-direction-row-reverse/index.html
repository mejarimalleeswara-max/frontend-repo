ABOUT VOLLEY BALL

Objective:The main goal in volleyball is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the opponent's court, while preventing the opponent from doing the same.

Teams and Players Each team has six players on the court at a time: three in the front row and three in the back row.

Scoring System Volleyball uses rally scoring, meaning a point is scored on every serve, regardless of which team served. Matches are usually best of five sets, with sets played to 25 points

Serving Each play begins with a serve from behind the end line. The server must hit the ball over the net into the opponent's court.

Rotations:After winning a serve back, players rotate clockwise before the next serve. This ensures everyone plays both front and back row positions.

FOOT BALL

Objective:The main goal is to score by getting the ball into the opponent's net, while preventing the other team from scoring.

Teams and Players -Each team has 11 players, including one goalkeeper and ten outfield players (defenders, midfielders, and forwards).

Match Duration:A standard match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two 45-minute halves, with a short halftime break.

The Ball:The game is played with a spherical ball that players move primarily using their feet, though headers and chest control are allowed.

Field of Play -Matches are played on a rectangular grass or artificial turf field, with goals at each end.

Fouls and Free Kicks - Physical contact or unfair play (like tripping, pushing, or handball) results in a free kick or penalty kick for the opposing team.

THROW Ball

Objective The main goal in throwball is to throw the ball over the net and make it land inside the opponent's court, while preventing the other team from returning it.

Teams and Players :Each team has seven players on the court and five substitutes.

Court and Net: The throwball court measures 12.2 meters wide and 18.3 meters long, and the net height is 2.2 meters for women and 2.4 meters for men

Serving :The game starts with a serve thrown from behind the end line. The ball must cross the net without touching it.

Rally Scoring :A point is awarded on every serve, whether by fault or successful play. A match is usually played as the best of three sets, each set played to 25 points.
