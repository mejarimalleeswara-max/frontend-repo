About cars

  • A car (automobile) is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transporting people, typically powered by an internal combustion engine or an electric motor.
  • Common types include sedans, SUVs, hatchbacks, coupes, convertibles, trucks, and electric vehicles
  • Cars can run on gasoline, diesel, electricity, hybrid systems (gas + electric), or alternative fuels like hydrogen.
  • New cars include smart features like GPS navigation, touchscreens, AI-assisted driving, and autonomous (self-driving) technology.
  • The automobile industry supports millions of jobs globally and drives innovation in manufacturing and technology.

About bikes

A bike, or bicycle, is a two-wheeled vehicle powered by human pedaling or, in some cases, an electric motor.

Common types include road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, BMX, touring bikes, and electric bikes

Key components are the frame, wheels, pedals, chain, gears, brakes, handlebar, and saddle (seat).

Bikes produce no emissions, making them eco-friendly and great for reducing air pollution and traffic congestion.

Riders should wear helmets, reflective clothing, and use lights or bells to stay safe, especially at night.
