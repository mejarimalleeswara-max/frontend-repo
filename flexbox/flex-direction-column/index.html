abdul kalam

Full Name: Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.

Birth: Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.

Education: Studied Physics at St. Joseph’s College, Tiruchirappalli, and Aerospace Engineering at the Madras Institute of Technology (MIT).

Scientific Career: Worked with the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Missile Man of India: Played a key role in developing India’s ballistic missile and space rocket technology, including the Agni and Prithvi missiles.

Gandhi

Full Name: Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi.

Birth: Born on 2 October 1869 in Porbandar, Gujarat, India.

Education: Studied law in London, England, and became a barrister.

Work in South Africa: Fought against racial discrimination and developed his philosophy of Satyagraha (non-violent resistance).

Return to India: Came back to India in 1915 and joined the Indian freedom struggle.

Ambedkar

Full Name: Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar.

Birth: Born on 14 April 1891 in Mhow (now in Madhya Pradesh), India.

Family Background: Belonged to a Dalit (Scheduled Caste) family and faced social discrimination from a young age.

Education: Was highly educated — earned doctorates from Columbia University (USA) and the London School of Economics (UK).

Political Leader: Founded the Scheduled Castes Federation and played a key role in Indian politics during the freedom struggle.
